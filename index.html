Welcome to the Halloween Workers Union!

Join Our Spooky Crew for Better Working "Afterlife"!

Are you a ghost, zombie, vampire, or werewolf looking for fair treatment in the afterlife? Look no further! The Halloween Workers Union is here to ensure that even the undead enjoy decent working conditions on All Hallows' Eve.

Imagine a world where zombies get breaks between brain hunts, vampires have safe coffins to sleep in during daylight hours, and ghosts are guaranteed haunting spots with good visibility and ectoplasmic amenities.

At the Halloween Workers Union, we're all about unionizing the supernatural for a howling good time on Halloween night. Just like our living counterparts, we believe in fair wages (paid in candy, of course!), breaks for potion refreshment, and cackling rights for witches!
